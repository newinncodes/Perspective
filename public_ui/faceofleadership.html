About this piece

The idea for this piece originated in Dec 2022 after I noticed that the executive announcements on my LinkedIn page appeared uniformly male and white

This fired up my curiosity to analyse the optic diversity of leadership teams2 in the UK tech scene - and represent what I found in a visually impactful way through their faces

The piece will continually refresh each day as more data is collated into the project and the algorithms are updated

This piece was created in Python + MongoDB; with smatterings of Django, html, javascript and css

This is not intended to be a thought-leadership piece on diversity in UK tech. If that's what you're after, then check out Tech Nation's report on Diversity and Inclusion in the UK
