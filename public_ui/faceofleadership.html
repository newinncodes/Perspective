About this piece

The idea for this piece originated in Dec 2022 after I noticed that the executive announcements on my LinkedIn page appeared uniformly male and white

This fired up my curiosity to analyse the optic diversity of exec teams2 in the UK tech scene - and represent what I found in a visual way through their faces

This piece was created in Python + MongoDB + Django; with smatterings of html, javascript and css

The piece will continually refresh each day as more data is collated into the project and the algorithm evolves

This is not intended to be a thought-leadership piece on diversity in UK tech. If that's what you're after, then check out Tech Nation's report on Diversity and Inclusion in the UK
